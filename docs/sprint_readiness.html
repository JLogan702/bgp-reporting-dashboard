How is this report calculated?
Sprint Readiness is calculated as:
(Ready Stories ÷ Total Sprint Stories) × 100

• “Ready” = stories in 'To Do' or 'Ready for Development'
• For Engineering - Product, only 'Ready for Development' counts

Thresholds:
🟢 ≥ 80% = Ready
🟡 50–79% = Caution
🔴 < 50% = Risk

Sources: Scrum.org, Agile Alliance
